Unique Designs Professional Services, Inc. (UDP) is a HUBZone certified, minority-owned small business. Since 2002, UDP has provided educational and business consulting services to individuals, for-profit, nonprofit, faith-based, and government entities. UDP is a full-service management firm that specializes in logistics, communications, documentation management, professional development, and research and technical writing.
